The campaign

A shoppable stage in the sky.
One balloon. Infinite stages.

The platform stays the same. The show on it changes every time. One week it's a laser tag arena, the next it's a five a side pitch, then a retro gaming lounge, then a marketplace floating over the city. Each one streams live on ebayLIVE, and every object in frame is biddable and tap to buy the second it appears.

The card break people expect from eBay becomes one segment of a much bigger live show, not the whole idea. That is the unlock. A single build that eBay can run all year, with a new reason to tune in every time.
