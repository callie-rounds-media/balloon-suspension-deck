**The situation:** A creator is pitching eBay, specifically eBay Live (eBay's live-shopping / live-auction platform), on a flagship marketing spectacle. Your job is to design AND write the entire deck. Invent the headlines, the section names, the copy, the narrative flow, all of it. Be bold. **The facts you're working from:**
- The concept: a real, full-size NFL football game, played on a platform suspended beneath a hot air balloon wrapped in full eBay Live branding, thousands of feet up, above the clouds. Players, cheerleaders, goalposts, the works. It has never been done.
- The commerce hook: woven through the game is a live trading-card break (opening sealed sports-card packs and auctioning the pulls in real time) streaming on eBay Live. - The ask: get eBay Live to take the meeting and build it.

**Brand identity (this is eBay, honor it):**
- Light theme. eBay's four brand colors: red #E53238, blue #0064D2, yellow #F5AF02, green #86B817, used with restraint. Blue is the primary accent.
- Recreate the "eBay Live" wordmark: lowercase "ebay" with each letter in one of the four brand colors, followed by a small red rounded "LIVE" badge in white.
- Clean modern typeface (Sora or Inter). Premium, spacious, confident. No dark purple. Avoid anything that looks like a generic template.

**Design bar:** High-end agency keynote meets Apple product page. Large confident type, generous whitespace, editorial rhythm, subtle scroll-reveal animation, tasteful depth, cinematic image treatments (photos on a deep navy backdrop). It should feel expensive and calm.

**Images:** Generate and place real, photorealistic images yourself throughout the deck, the hero (a hot air balloon in full eBay Live livery suspending an NFL football field high above the clouds at golden hour, players and cheerleaders on it), the concept sections, and any cinematic scene imagery. **Technical:** Fully responsive, no horizontal scroll on mobile. Self-contained. Smooth scroll, gentle fade/rise on scroll into view. Clean, well-organized component structure so it is easy to edit later.
